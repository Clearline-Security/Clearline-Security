**Operational security consulting for journalists, activists, and human rights defenders.**

## What We Do

Clearline Security provides threat modeling, operational security assessments, and secure communications consulting for individuals and organizations operating in high-risk environments.

Our work is built on a single premise:

> Most security failures are not technical. They are operational. We address both.

We work with:

- Investigative journalists and independent reporters
- Human rights defenders and civil society organizations
- Activists and political dissidents
- Non-governmental organizations
- Whistleblowers and legal counsel supporting them

---

## The SCOPE Framework

The foundation of our methodology is the **SCOPE Framework** (Security-Centered Operational Protection & Exposure) — a people-first threat modeling methodology developed specifically for high-risk individuals.

Unlike enterprise frameworks designed for software systems and corporate networks, SCOPE models the entire operational ecosystem: people, communications, devices, identities, physical environments, and operational routines.

## Core Principles

- Protect people before technology
- Treat confidential sources as the highest-value assets
- Tools reduce risk — architecture prevents catastrophe
- Proportional, practical controls that users can actually sustain
- Reassess continuously — threat models are not static documents
